About Us

At Musicalaya, We Aim To Make Music Education Affordable and Accessible in India, and To fill the country with Music.

Musicalaya started off as an idea when we came across various music schools, where students are paying a heavy amount to learn music. Not, only is the price for Music Education very high, but the quantity of lessons is also very less in comparision to the fees that is charged.
This makes the music learning process longer, frustrating and expensive.
So, we started wondering how we can make your learning experience Budget friendly, Faster and Efficient.
This is where the idea behind Musicalaya was born. Well, how we do that? You need to wait for a while.

But until then, We at Musicalaya, promise to bring you High Quality Music Courses at an Affordable Price with more number of Lessons.
